Lenso — About, Vision, and Plans

About Lenso
We built Lenso so people can share authentic experiences and help others feel like they were there. It’s a community-first space where short stories, moments, photos, and videos are easy to post and delightful to explore.

Our Vision
- Empower anyone to capture and share meaningful experiences.
- Help people discover real perspectives across cultures and places.
- Use AI responsibly to enhance (not replace) human stories.
- Keep the community welcoming, safe, and positive.

Guiding Principles
- Authenticity over virality: Real moments matter most.
- Safety by design: Smart moderation and simple controls.
- Creator respect: Clear ownership, attribution, and choice.
- Performance and privacy: Fast, reliable, and minimal data collection.

Near‑Term Roadmap (0–3 months)
- Rich posting: Multiple photos/videos per post, simple editing.
- Better discovery: Trending tags, smarter search, and curated collections.
- Social basics: Comments, reactions, and lightweight profiles.
- AI assists: Title/summary/hashtags suggestions with clear user controls.
- Moderation: Automated checks plus user reporting and appeals.
- Mobile polish: Improved responsiveness and touch interactions.

Mid‑Term Plans (3–9 months)
- Real‑time features: Live rooms and event threads.
- Collaboration: Co‑authored posts and series.
- Creator tools: Drafts, scheduled posts, analytics.
- Trust and safety: Community guidelines, transparency reports.
- Integrations: S3 and CDN hardening, export/share options.

Future Ideas (Exploration)
- Location layers with privacy‑first controls.
- AI copilots for multilingual captions and accessibility (alt text, transcripts).
- Offline capture with sync.
- Thematic channels and community‑run collections.

How We’ll Measure Progress
- Quality of experiences shared (not just quantity).
- Community safety and satisfaction.
- Creator success and retention.
- Performance and reliability.

Thanks for building Lenso with us.
